The journey behind building the first design system for 3S Money

The UVU Student Mobile App project addressed a critical challenge for Utah Valley University, a large institution with over 44,000 students: a fragmented and outdated digital ecosystem that burdened students with disconnected systems for essential tasks. As Creative Lead, I directed a team of three designers to reimagine UVU's digital experience, designing an intuitive mobile interface that unified key services like tuition payments and class registration. Our solution transformed institutional pain points into a seamless student journey, setting a new benchmark for digital innovation at UVU.

outdated mobile experience

Past university apps failed by not solving real student problems. Partnering with UVU’s Digital Transformation team and Student Council, we validated a key opportunity: a unified platform for schedules, deadlines, and alerts—a feature prospective students now expect from tech-forward institutions.

disjointed desktop applications
